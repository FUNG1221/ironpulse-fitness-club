FAQ

Questions Before You Train?

Do you offer a free trial?
Yes. New members can request a free trial session through the contact form.
Is IronPulse beginner friendly?
Absolutely. Our coaches help beginners train safely and confidently.
Do you offer personal training?
Yes. We offer one-on-one coaching through our Personal Training and Elite plans.
Are classes included in membership?
Pro and Elite members receive unlimited group classes. Basic members receive limited monthly access.
Do you provide nutrition guidance?
Yes. Pro and Elite plans include nutrition support to help with body composition goals.
What should I bring for my first workout?
Bring workout clothes, training shoes, a water bottle, and a ready-to-work mindset.
Do you have boxing classes?
Yes. Boxing Fitness is one of our most popular programs.
Can I cancel my membership?
Yes. Memberships are flexible and cancellation support is available through our front desk.
